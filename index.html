Cute Animals Rescue

Cute Animals Rescue is a rescue that saves cute animals from being killed, abused, and/or not being nurtured properly.

The main three cute animals we rescue the most are:

  • Munchkin Cats
  • Miniture Pigs
  • Pomeranian Dogs

    • Munchkin Cats

    These cats have small paws, but the muscles in the paws are strong. They being so small does not affect their health or lifestyle. These cats are socialble, docile, and affectionate cats. But munchkin cats are brave and noble also.
